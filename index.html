ABOUT

APPROACH

MY WORK STARTS WITH MUSIC. I USE UKRAINIAN ALBUMS AND ARTISTS AS A BASE FOR VISUAL EXPLORATION — TAKING LYRICS, MOODS, AND SOUNDS AND TRANSLATING THEM INTO DESIGN. I WORK WITH UNBALANCED GRIDS, RAW TEXTURES, AND UNUSUAL COMPOSITIONS TO BUILD SOMETHING THAT FEELS HONEST, IMPERFECT, AND ALIVE.

EACH PROJECT IS A CHANCE TO IMPROVE, PUSH MY ABILITIES, AND CREATE VISUAL EXPERIENCES THAT STICK — SOMETHING YOU FEEL, NOT JUST SEE.
